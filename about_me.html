Designer, artist and programmer.

Hi, I'm a physicist-turned-artist who works across genre and medium. I like to work on art/design projects that involve computer/internet/digital media as a tool to create a new immersive experience or to provide a fresh perpective.

I have also been involved with theater for most of my life, and thus I frequently act, direct, write and now also design for the stage.

I graduated with a Bachelor's in Physics and Humanitties from Massachusetts Institue of Technology in 2013. And currently I'm at Harvard Graduate School of Design pursuing a Master's in Design and Technology.
